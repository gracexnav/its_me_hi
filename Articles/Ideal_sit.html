An Ideal Situation

He walked in, and I was so shocked by his beauty. He was tall and had golden brown skin. His hair is wavy, and his dimples form when he smiles. The evening sunlight makes his skin smooth. He has an amazing fashion sense. Oh, how I would love to see him in a tailored suit or in a casual shirt. Of course, this did not happen, and this might not happen at all in the future since this is an ideal situation. Ideal things do not exist. But one can hope. So why do people have an ideal type?

Well, let's say that you have eaten all kinds of ice cream flavors, and you have a favorite among those flavors. You fix this flavor as your ideal type, and when you eat ice cream next time, you compare it with your ideal type. You like the ice cream you ate if it was closer to your ideal type, or else you would not. I think this is the basic reason why people have ideal types. It helps to keep a standard and compare it. So, what is my ideal type?

Well, Tall, black wavy hair, golden brown skin, brown eyes, fashionable (having dimples is a bonus point), serious but has a pinch of cuteness (Disclaimer: I am not describing a dish). This is just one layer of cake, 'Appearance'. There is another layer of cake called 'personality'. Being considerate is a must, a good listener, and gives respect, and of course, is sincere. That's all I ask. If he is smart, then it will be the cream that finishes the cake. If he can sing well or has a deep voice, then it would be the chocolate on the cake.
